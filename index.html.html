Preg Go Veda Proposal

Personal Brand + Clinic + Educational Platform( Blogs..etc)

Inclusive of Website, Mobile Apps, Hosting, Push Notifications & Marketing Modules

1. Understanding Client Expectations

Objective: To build a premium, future-ready, and holistic “Personal Brand + Ayurvedic Clinic + Educational Platform” for Pregnancy, Fertility, Women’s Wellness, and Postpartum Care, under the brand Preg Go Veda.

The platform must simultaneously support: - Personal brand growth for Dr. Deepa Sandeep - Clinic reputation and online presence - Educational content publishing - Patient engagement - Digital product sales - Multi-platform accessibility (Website + Mobile Apps)

1.1 What the Client Wants to Achieve

  • Establish strong online presence with a visually appealing, calming, Ayurveda-inspired digital identity.
  • Grow personal brand authority as a trusted fertility expert and Ayurvedic practitioner.
  • Increase patient consultations through an easy, quick, and mobile-friendly booking experience.
  • Educate users using blogs, tips, fertility articles, hormone balance guidance, lifestyle routines, etc.
  • Showcase authentic patient success stories that build trust while maintaining privacy.
  • Create a long-term digital asset that can scale into courses, ebooks, postpartum programs, and more.

1.2 Required Experience on the Platform

  • A warm, welcoming homepage that reflects the brand value: “Helping couples achieve natural fertility through Ayurveda”.
  • A clean, minimal, nature-based design that instills trust and emotional comfort.
  • User-friendly navigation for women seeking fertility, pregnancy, postpartum, and hormone support.
  • Seamless consultation booking via:
    • Website form
    • WhatsApp direct chat
    • Calendly appointment scheduling
  • Ability for the doctor/team to post regular Ayurvedic and fertility-based articles.
  • Easy for patients to explore treatments, programs, and success outcomes.

1.3 Branding Expectations

  • Color Palette: Soft sage green, beige, pastel pink, off-white, subtle gold.
  • Design Style: Clean, modern, feminine, nature-based Ayurvedic visuals (leaves, herbs, calm textures).
  • Tone: Supportive, empathetic, trustworthy, medically sound yet emotionally warm.
  • Typography: Soft rounded professional fonts to maintain a gentle, wellness-oriented feel.

1.4 Detailed Page Requirements

Home Page
  • Warm welcome message with soft visuals.
  • Clear mission statement about natural fertility healing.
  • Quick booking CTAs:
    • Book Consultation
    • Join WhatsApp Group
    • Download Free Guide
  • Showcase specializations: Fertility • Postpartum • PCOS/PCOD • Hormonal Balance • Men’s Wellness.
  • Instagram feed integration for social proof.
  • Success stories or testimonials preview.
About Page
  • Doctor’s journey, mission, passion, and philosophy.
  • Qualifications and experience in Ayurveda + Fertility.
  • Photos of the doctor and/or clinic (optional).
  • The story behind launching Preg Go Veda.
Services Page

Each service must include: detailed description, duration, benefits, eligibility, and “Book Now” button.

  • Online/Offline Consultations
  • 21-Day or 45-Day Fertility Enhancement Program
  • PCOS/PCOD Ayurvedic Management
  • Postpartum Ayurvedic Recovery Program
  • Hormonal Balance Therapy
  • Men’s Reproductive Health Support
  • Workshops/Webinars (live or recorded)
Blog / Articles Section
  • Regular Ayurvedic tips for couples and women
  • Fertility myths vs facts
  • Ayurvedic diet for pregnancy prep
  • Hormonal balance and lifestyle
  • PCOS/PCOD education
  • Postpartum healing routines
  • Reusing Instagram captions as blog content
Testimonials & Success Stories
  • Real stories of couples who conceived naturally.
  • Patient identities must remain confidential.
  • Structured “Before → Ayurvedic Approach → Outcome” format.
  • Text and optional video testimonials.
Contact / Booking Page
  • Simple consultation booking form
  • WhatsApp button for direct contact
  • Google Maps integration for clinic location
  • Email, phone, and business hours
  • Optional Calendly integration
Shop / Resources Page (Optional – Phase 2)
  • Downloadable guides (free or paid)
  • Fertility eBooks
  • Digital products and healing programs
  • Affiliate products such as herbal supplements

1.5 Platform Expectations (Web + Apps)

  • Both website and mobile app should share a unified design language.
  • Patients should be able to access blogs, services, booking, testimonials from both platforms.
  • Push notifications should keep patients engaged with new blog posts, programs, reminders.
  • Admin panel should be easy for the client to manage blogs, services, and updates.

1.6 Priority Goals for the Client

  • Get a professional digital presence quickly.
  • Increase patient conversions through simple booking flows.
  • Consistently publish content to build credibility.
  • Offer digital programs in the future.
  • Reach more people through mobile apps.
